# Welcome to dbForge 

[dbForge](https://www.devart.com/dbforge/) is an AI-powered ecosystem that simplifies and automates database lifecycle management, covering all of its stages from design and development to testing, deployment, and administration.

dbForge comprises multidatabase solutions, deeply RDBMS-specific and AI-powered IDEs, standalone task-oriented applications, and versatile add-ins for SSMS and Visual Studio. All of these products are consistently designed, highly intuitive, and ready to elevate the user's capabilities from day one.
